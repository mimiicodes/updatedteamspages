Our Executives

This exceptional set of great minds have over the year rowed the boat of ECX 2.0. From making major decisions on how the edition is to be run, to planning and organizing webinars, these people have made great contributions and sacrifice to this edition of Engineering Career Expo and we are grateful for them.They are ECX stars, the light to our sun.

Sodiq Akinjobi

Chairman

ECX has shown me that you can cause real change in people if you care about their journey. I have seen people go from nothing to empowering themselves with skills that will shape their future. ECX has also shown me that the youths of today can withstand any challenge thrown at them. I've seen students build amazing things in the middle of a pandemic and in spite of all the hardship in the country. Working with my team has been a blessing. From the good days to the bad days when things aren't working as we expect it to but the determination and perseverance of everyone helped us pull through. Best team ever!

Daniel Oni

Head of Media Team

Great. Amazing.

Kodinnanma Ezike

Head, Finance and Quality Control

ECX is a great platform for teaching students both soft and hard skills that they will need after school. It's been great working with ECX. I have learnt a lot about team work and collaboration. I have also learnt to accept people's opinions and work smoothly with them. One major challenge we experienced was funding, it would be really great if organizations can support ECX. Moving forward we imparted a lot of people and gave them skills which they are working with presently. In the future, I hope that we can have more students and that people see the importance of gaining these skills.
