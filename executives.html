Our Executives

This exceptional set of great minds have over the year rowed the boat of ECX 2.0. From making major decisions on how the edition is to be run, to planning and organizing webinars, these people have made great contributions and sacrifice to this edition of Engineering Career Expo and we are grateful for them.They are ECX stars, the light to our sun.

Sodiq Akinjobi

Chairman

ECX has shown me that you can cause real change in people if you care about their journey. ECX has also shown me that the youths of today can withstand any challenge thrown at them. Working with my team has been a blessing. From the good days to the bad days when things weren't working as we expected them to but the determination and perseverance of everyone helped us pull through. Best team ever!

Daniel Oni

Head of Media Team

A great experience! It was amazing

Kodinnanma Ezike

Head, Finance and Quality Control

ECX is a great platform for teaching students both soft and hard skills. It's been great working with ECX. I have learnt a lot about team work and collaboration. I have also learnt to accept people's opinions and work smoothly with them. One major challenge we experienced was funding, it would be really great if organizations can support ECX. I hope that people see the importance of gaining these skills.

Douye Tungbulu

Head of Media Team

I won't ever forget the lessons I learnt from being part of ECX, working as a team even when there were ups and downs, learning from each other has really helped and improved me. A family I won't ever forget!

Abubakar Yusuf

Head, Training Team

As the Head of Training, skills such as time and people management and effective execution were some of the things I picked up. Glad I had a wonderful team and amazing mates at Command Centre which made collaboration easier and overcoming challenges less overwhelming. I got to see the effect our decisions had on individuals and I would say it was both amazing and positive to watch them grow. I feel fulfilled and glad I joined.

Ifihanagbara Olusheye

Mustapha Raji

Deputy, Logistics Team

Well, ECX 1.0 was good, ECX 2.0 is better, in the next few years, if ECX organizers are filled with intelligent, selfless people, ECX will be great. There are lots of things to dive into and lots of students to help.
